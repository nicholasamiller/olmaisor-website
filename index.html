Two campsites: Ngiliriti and Ngriki

Ol Maisor has two campsites: Ngiliriti and Ngriki.
Ngriki: a bush camp is in a large beautiful acacia and olive grove, with five twin safari tents, a functional bush kitchen and mess area.
Ngiliriti: close to our main gate in a fully fenced secure compound, with large shady acacias, a bathroom and shower block, laundry and a camp kitchen/mess area.

Tents

We have large canvas safari tents available that are fully made up with beds and bedding. The tents can comfortable fit 2 king single beds and a mattress can be added for kids. You are also welcome to bring your our tent when staying in the Ngriki campsite.

Staff

We have a great team on hand to help at both sites during your Stay. Nick our guide will be available to help plan your stay and help facilitate any activites. We also have a day and night guard who will help in washing up, heating water for your showers and keeping the fire lit. We are fully self catered but if you would like a cook on site to help in meal preperation this can easily be arranged.

Amenities

Each camp has a mess area set up with tables and chairs. We supply fire wood and washing water as well as a bbq and charcoal. There is a toilet and shower block at both the site. For large bookings, we can set up an extra toilet tent.

Introduction

Camp on Ol Maisor Farm

Ol Maisor is a working cattle and cropping farm in Northern Laikipia. We have an abundance of Wildlife and we encourage guests to explore the property on self driven game drives. We have maps available and Nick is always will to share his expertise and knowledge of Ol Maisor. We also welcome guests to engage with life on the farm through a training session with our bloodhounds, a camel safari or a visit to see our beautiful boran cattle. We have a number of beautiful dams perfect for swimming, fishing or enjoying a sundowner at.

Ngriki Campsite

This bush camp is in a large beautiful acacia and olive grove. We have 5 twin safari tents, fitted with comfortable beds and bedding. We are a fully self-catered camp but there is a functional bush kitchen and mess area and a cook can be arranged to come and prepare your meals for you.

$40 USD per person per night.

$20 USD per child under 12 per night.

Ngiliriti Campsite

This campsite is close to our main gate in a fully fenced secure compound. Large shady acacias mean you can always find a good spot to pitch your tent. On site is a bathroom and shower block, including large sinks for laundry and a camp kitchen/mess area. Firewood and drinking water is also available and we have a small farm produce shop on site.

$15 USD per person per night.

Get to Know Us

Ol Maisor is a large cattle and agricultural property in Laikipia, Kenya. The same family has owned and run it for three generations. It currently produces beef, flowers and maize. Artisan cheese and honey are also producted on the property. The farm has invested to protect local wildlife, with over 30 kilometers of hand-built elephant corridors, dedicated staff to track lion prides and anti-poaching security. Hundreds of large grazers such as giraffe, lions, buffalo, zebra and impala share the farm with our boran cattle.

Getting there

You have two options for getting to Ol Maisor camp:
  • Drive and park at your camp site. The camp is around two and a half hours from Nanuki and about the same from Nakuru, by mostly dirt roads.
  • Fly to Ol Maisor airstrip. We will 4WD you and your stuff to the camps, which takes about ten minutes. The strip is well-maintained and frequently used. The surrounding land is cleared of vegetation so any wild life is visible. Sometimes a hanger and fuel is available.
