Two campsites

Ol Maisor has two camps available and we offer them on a single booking basis so the sites wil always be exclusively yours.
The Ngriki is a large shady glade in a acacia and olive forrest. It is perfect for large groups and families with plenty of space for active kids to run around. The site often has Elephants wandering past and a huge variety of bird-life inhabit the site.
Urejesho is hidden in the bush on the banks of one of our beautiful dams. It is a beautiful site to sit back and watch the wildlife come into drink and the crested cranes come to roost. The dam is also a lovely spot to swim and cool off after a hot day.

Tents

We have large canvas safari tents available that are fully made up with beds and bedding. The tents can comfortable fit 2 king single beds and a mattress can be added for kids. You are also welcome to bring your our tent when staying in the Ngriki campsite.

Staff

We have a great team on hand to help at both sites during your Stay. Nick our guide will be available to help plan your stay and help facilitate any activites. We also have a day and night guard who will help in washing up, heating water for your showers and keeping the fire lit. We are fully self catered but if you would like a cook on site to help in meal preperation this can easily be arranged.

Amenities

Each camp has a mess tent set up with tables, chairs and crockery. We supply fire wood and washing water as well as a bbq and charcoal. There is a toilet and shower block at bothe sites and for large bookings an extra toilet tent can be set up.

Introduction

Camp on Ol Maisor Farm

Ol Maisor is a working cattle and cropping farm in Northern Laikipia. We have an abundance of Wildlife and we encourage guests to explore the property on self driven game drives. We have maps available and Nick is always will to share his expertise and knowledge of Ol Maisor. We also welcome guests to engage with life on the farm through a training session with our bloodhounds, a camel safari or a visit to see our beautiful boran cattle. We have a number of beautiful dams perfect for swimming, fishing or enjoying a sundowner at.
